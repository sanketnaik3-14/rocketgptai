About Us

Rocket Health is a mental health company that provides online therapy sessions for people from Monday to Sunday from 9 am to 8 pm. We believe that everyone deserves access to mental health support and our mission is to provide high-quality therapy services that are accessible, affordable, and convenient.

Our team of licensed therapists is dedicated to helping people achieve mental wellness and improve their quality of life. We use evidence-based practices and take a client-centered approach to therapy, which means that we tailor our services to meet the unique needs and goals of each individual.

Our Therapy

We offer a range of therapy services, including individual therapy, couples therapy, and family therapy. Our therapists are trained to help with a variety of mental health issues, such as anxiety, depression, trauma, and relationship problems.

We use a variety of therapeutic approaches, including cognitive-behavioral therapy, solution-focused therapy, and mindfulness-based therapy. Our therapy sessions are conducted online, which means that you can access our services from anywhere with an internet connection.

At Rocket Health, we believe that everyone can benefit from therapy, and we are committed to helping people improve their mental health and well-being.
